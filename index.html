About us

HIPHOP WORLD is a reference in the hiphop culture. This website has been createdby a fan of hip-hop culture, music and dance .

HIPHOP WORLD will keep you informed of all the most famous upcoming events of the b-boying World (Red Bull BC One, Silverback, DPC Jam, Battle of the Year, Fusion concept, CrashFest, Undisputed….). A link to the ticket sales

will also be provided for each shared event. You are looking for information about hip-hop culture? You will find all the information you need on this website and more. Go to our “Culture” page so you will discover the origins of the dance, the important figures of this culture, and the vocabulary specific to hip-hop.

HIPHOP WORLD also provides detailed presentations of all the best current dancers.
You will have access to their professional background, titles, dance style, philosophy and more.
You are a fan of the Red Bull BC One all-stars ‘crew? You will find all the information about your favorite dancers.

Some of the best hip-hop and Break beats/mixtapes of the moment will also be shared every month through posts.
